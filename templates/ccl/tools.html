Tools for researchers

Towi assessment

The Towi assessment allows to detect difficulties in certain cognitive abilities. It is based on standardized neuropsychological tests and the validity of its results was confirmed by an independent research team at Towi. Researchers can apply it to children and get their results without cost. Read more.

Towi training

The Towi training games are designed to help develop cognitive skills such as attention, memory and executive functions. This training is available for research purposes.

Open database

The database with information on the assessments and metrics of the training games of all children who have used Towi is freely accessible to researchers.

Support in data analysis with artificial intelligence

The Towi team can support the analysis of information with the help of artificial intelligence models, providing advice or the tools that the researcher requires to examine their data.
