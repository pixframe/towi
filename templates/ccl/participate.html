Participation Process

To participate in a research project using Towi as a tool, follow these steps:

  1. Register in the portal and send us your proposal through the form that you will find at the bottom of this page.
  2. Once your proposal is reviewed and accepted, you will receive an Excel format by email. Fill it with the general information of the children who will participate in the research and send it back.
  3. Approximately one week after sending your form, you will receive the Towi download link, as well as the user names and passwords of the children you included in the format.
  4. Download Towi in the devices you will use for research, and test its operation with your account before using it with children.
  5. Apply the evaluation and/or Towi training to the children according to the needs of your research. Check the results and progress of the children in the monitoring portal, available at: www.towi.com.mx
  6. Once your project is finished, share the results in this portal through the "load Project" button that you will find on the home page. You can also share your research protocol and partial progress while your research is ongoing.
